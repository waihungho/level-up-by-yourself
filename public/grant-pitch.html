The Product

An Idle RPG That Plays Itself — On Chain

Players summon AI-powered agents with unique stat distributions across 50 dimensions, watch them grow autonomously every day, train them through battles, and compete for global dominance — all from their Seeker phone.

🧬

50-Dimension Agent DNA

Every agent is born with unique stats across Physical, Mental, Social, Spiritual, and Technical categories. No two agents are alike — each is a one-of-a-kind digital being.

🤖

AI-Powered Daily Growth

Claude AI generates unique growth narratives every day. Agents evolve based on their role archetype — medieval warriors grow Physical stats, future agents grow Technical.

⚔️

Async PvP Battles

Challenge other players' agents in dimension-based combat. 3 battles per agent per day — no need for both players online. Strategy meets idle mechanics.

🏆

Global Leaderboard

Compete on the rankings with your W/L record. Climb the ladder by building the strongest team of agents across all dimensions.

💰

SOL-Powered Summoning

Free summon every 7 days, or pay SOL via Mobile Wallet Adapter for instant summons. On-chain transactions, zero friction on Seeker.

📱

Seeker-Native Build

ARM64-only APK at just 21MB. Optimized for the Seeker hardware with Solana MWA — no QR codes, no browser redirects, pure native feel.
